Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of the Google Play Store to reveal patterns in app performance. I analyzed the impact of variables like app size, category, and pricing on user engagement, delivering key insights that define what makes an application rank at the top of the market.

# Google Play Store: App Market Analysis
An analysis of the Google Play Store ecosystem to identify what drives app success. 

## Key Insights
Data Transformation Excellence: I successfully standardized critical variables such as 'Price' and 'App Size' by cleaning raw data and converting data types to ensure accurate statistical analysis.

Category Dynamics: My analysis of distribution by category revealed that sectors like 'Family' and 'Games' dominate the volume of applications, while niche categories show distinct rating behaviors.

Pricing vs. Quality: I investigated the correlation between app pricing models and average ratings, identifying how free applications achieve greater reach while facing different user retention challenges compared to paid apps.

Engagement Indicators: I discovered that applications with a higher number of reviews tend to consolidate more stable ratings, suggesting that user engagement is the primary engine of success in the store.
